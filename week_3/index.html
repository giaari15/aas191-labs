Lab #1: Hello World!

Map of Westwood + places I am familiar with

My name is Gia. I am a rising 3rd year Statistics and Data Science and Asian American Studies double major with a Digital Humanities Minor. I love fashion and film. My favorite designer is Yohji Yamamoto. My favorite director is either Hideaki Anno or Wong Kar Wai, but my favorite movie is Suspiria (1977). My goals as a critical digital map maker are to provide a congregated space for marginalized voices throughout the LA area and to heed way for spatial justice. As gentrification and encampment sweepings continue, I hope to be able to provide further insight of the humanness of those directly impacted, and the ways in which we continue to resist. People are not statistics. Laws, politicians, and the wealthy cannot define the people as unworthy of our human rights. Community prevails!
